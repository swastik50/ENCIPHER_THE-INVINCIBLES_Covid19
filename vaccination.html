Eigibility for vaccination

In the initial phase, COVID-19 vaccine will be provided to the priority group:

  1. Health care workers
  2. front line workers

The second phase vaccinations, started March 1st, 2021 has allowed all Indians above the age of 60 and those between 45 and 59 with comorbidities to be vaccinationated.

The comorbidities that make a person eligible for vaccinations are listed below:

  1. Heart Failure with hospital admission in past one year
  2. Post Cardiac Transplant/Left Ventricular Assist Device (LVAD)
  3. Significant Left ventricular systolic dysfunction (LVEF <40%)
  4. Moderate or Severe Valvular Heart Disease
  5. Decompensated cirrhosis
  6. Kidney/ Liver/ Hematopoietic stem cell transplant: Recipient/On wait-list
  7. End Stage Kidney Disease on haemodialysis/ CAPD
